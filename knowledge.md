# UNILINK FAQ

## How do I post a 'Lost & Found' item?
To post a lost or found item, go to the 'Lost & Found' section from the main dashboard and click the 'Add New' button.

## What is the 'Ride Share' feature?
The Ride Share feature allows students to find or offer rides to and from campus. You can search for existing rides or post your own trip.

## How do I request blood?
In an emergency, you can use the 'Blood Request' feature. Fill out the form with the patient's details, blood type, and hospital location.
