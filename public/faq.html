Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about UniLink's features including Blood Donation, Car Rental, Commute Pooling, and Lost & Found services.

What is UniLink and how does it work?

UniLink is a comprehensive campus platform that connects students through various services. After creating an account, you can access modules for blood donation requests, car rentals, commute pooling, and lost & found items.

How do I request blood donation through UniLink?

Navigate to the Blood Donation module, fill out the request form with required details like blood type, urgency, and location. The system will notify potential donors in your area who can respond to your request.

Can I rent a car through UniLink as a student?

Yes! The Car Rental module allows students to rent vehicles from other students or authorized campus partners. You can browse available cars, check rental terms, and book directly through the platform.

How does the commute pooling feature work?

Commute Pooling helps you find travel companions for your daily commute. You can either offer a ride or request one by specifying your route, timing, and number of available seats.

What should I do if I lose something on campus?

Use the Lost & Found module to report lost items or browse found items. Provide a detailed description and photos if possible. The system will notify you if a matching item is reported as found.

Is there a verification process for users?

Yes, all users are verified through their university email addresses. Additional verification may be required for certain features like car rental to ensure safety and reliability.

Are there any fees for using UniLink services?

Basic features like blood donation requests and lost & found are free. Car rentals and premium commute pooling options may involve fees, which are clearly displayed before confirmation.

How do I update my profile information?

Visit your Profile page where you can edit personal details, preferences, and notification settings. Keeping your profile updated ensures you get the most relevant matches for services.
